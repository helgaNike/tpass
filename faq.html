FAQ

  • How to use bonuses?

    Bonuses can be used on future purchases or exchanged for special offers within the service. Check the bonus usage conditions in your personal account.

  • How to earn bonuses?

    Bonuses are earned by performing certain actions on the platform, such as making purchases, registering, or inviting friends.

  • Are there any restrictions on using bonuses?

    Yes, bonuses have an expiration date. They must be used before the deadline or according to the terms of the promotions.

  • How to change personal information?

    Update your details in your profile under the "Settings" section. All changes are saved automatically.

  • Can bonuses be transferred to other users?

    No, bonuses are tied to your account and cannot be transferred to other users.

  • How to delete an account?

    To delete your account, contact customer support via email or chat on the platform.

  • What to do if bonuses are not credited?

    Check if you have fulfilled all the conditions for earning bonuses. If the issue persists, contact support.

  • How to activate a promo code?

    The promo code is entered during the checkout process. The discount will be applied automatically.

  • Can multiple promo codes be used?

    Usually, only one promo code can be used per purchase, but it depends on the promotion's terms.

  • Is there a mobile app to use the service?

    Yes, we have a mobile app available for both iOS and Android, allowing you to conveniently track your bonuses and activities
